# Great Southern Plumbing (SA) — Demo Website

A concept/demo website put together by Jamie (Angel Earthworks and Civil) to show what's possible.

---

## What this is

A fully working 4-page website for Great Southern Plumbing (SA) — built in the same style as the Angel Earthworks and Civil website. If the partnership goes ahead, both businesses can present with a coordinated, professional online presence.

## Open it

Open `index.html` in any web browser to view the site. All four pages link together properly:

- **index.html** — Homepage
- **services.html** — Detailed services
- **about.html** — About / credentials
- **contact.html** — Contact + quote form

---

## What's been built

### Pages

**Homepage** has a hero with the slogan, marquee of services scrolling across, three main service preview cards, the wastewater partnership callout with Angel Earthworks, six "why choose us" cards, stats band (38+ years, 20+ on Fleurieu, Licence PGE237715, SA owned), four-step process, service area cards focused on the southern Fleurieu, and a "photos coming soon" placeholder section.

**Services** page covers four detailed feature blocks (New Homes, Maintenance & Repairs, Commercial, Septic & Wastewater with Angel partnership), 12 specific service cards (gas, hot water, stormwater, rainwater, drain CCTV, sewer, pumps, backflow, roof/gutters, insurance work, emergency, renovations), and a pricing principles section.

**About** page tells Scott's story (trade since 1986, Fleurieu since 2006), four values, three credential cards (licence, insurance, registered business), and compliance stats.

**Contact** page has phone, email placeholder, postal address, service area, response time, hours, plumbing-specific quote request form, and operating hours grid.

### Technical bits done properly

- **Mobile responsive** — looks fine on phones (most customers will be on phones)
- **Schema markup** — structured data for Google so it knows this is a Plumber in Middleton SA
- **SEO** — meta descriptions, page titles, keywords for "plumber Fleurieu" type searches
- **Fast loading** — only 103KB total across all pages
- **No tracking, no ads** — clean professional site

## Why mirror Jamie's site?

Two reasons:

**Partnership signal.** When both businesses look professionally aligned, the partnership story is much more convincing to customers. They feel like a coordinated outfit — not two random tradies who happen to know each other.

**Cost and time.** Scott gets to skip all the design and structure decisions. The bones are already built and tested.
