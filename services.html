// 04 — Septic & Wastewater

Septics & Wastewater Treatment

Septic tanks, leach drains, biocycle systems, transpiration beds — the wastewater infrastructure most of the Fleurieu runs on.

New installs, replacements, upgrades, and repairs. Done to council and SA Health requirements and signed off properly. Decades of experience with all the common SA systems.

Systems We Work With

  • Conventional septic tanks & leach drains
  • Biocycle & aerobic treatment units (ATU/AWTS)
  • Transpiration beds & infiltrator systems
  • Pump wells & lift stations
  • Greywater diversion & reuse
  • Repairs, upgrades & replacement systems

End-to-End Installs

For full wastewater system installs we partner with Angel Earthworks and Civil to deliver the complete job — site assessment, council approval, excavation, tank, pipework, connection, and sign-off. One coordinated team, not three contractors to chase.
