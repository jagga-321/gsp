// The story

Plumbing the
Fleurieu since 2006.

Scott has been in the plumbing trade since 1986 — nearly four decades of hands-on experience across domestic, commercial, and septic work.

Originally from the northern parts of South Australia, Great Southern Plumbing relocated to the southern Fleurieu in 2006. Based in Middleton, working across Victor Harbor, Goolwa, Port Elliot, Encounter Bay and the surrounding areas.

From a leaky tap through to complete new home plumbing — the same standard of work goes into every job. Quality at a competitive price, done with a smile, and to WHS standards.
